## Getting Started

### 1.1 Welcome to QuickShop

QuickShop is a futuristic, scalable, AI-powered e-commerce platform designed to provide the best shopping experience with advanced features like AI recommendations, dynamic pricing, and seamless checkout processes.

### 1.2 System Requirements

**Web Browser:**
- Chrome 90+ or Firefox 88+ or Safari 14+ or Edge 90+
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled
- Minimum screen resolution: 1024x768

**Mobile Devices:**
- iOS 12+ or Android 6+
- Stable internet connection (minimum 3G)
- At least 100MB free storage

### 1.3 Creating an Account

1. Navigate to QuickShop website (https://quickshop.echelonxventures.org)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

## Customer Guide

### 2.1 Browsing Products

**Searching for Products:**
1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Type keywords (product name, brand, category)
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. Use filters to refine results:
   - Price range
   - Categories
   - Brands
   - Ratings
   - Product features

**Filtering Products:**
- Click on "Filters" button
- Select desired filters (price, category, brand, etc.)
- Click "Apply Filters"
- Results will update automatically

**Sorting Options:**
- Relevance (default)
- Price: Low to High
- Price: High to Low
- Customer Rating
- Newest Arrivals
- Best Selling

### 2.2 Product Details

**Viewing Product Information:**
- Click on any product image or name
- View multiple images by clicking on thumbnail images
- Read product description and specifications
- Check customer reviews and ratings
- View quantity in stock
- See similar products recommendations

### 2.3 Shopping Cart

**Adding Items to Cart:**
1. On product page, select desired options (size, color, quantity)
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. A notification will confirm the addition
4. Cart icon will show item count

**Managing Cart:**
- Click cart icon in header to view cart
- Adjust quantities using plus/minus buttons
- Remove items using the trash icon
- Apply discount codes if available
- Click "Proceed to Checkout" to continue

### 2.4 Checkout Process

**Step 1: Shipping Information**
1. Log in to your account
2. Enter shipping address or select saved address
3. Choose shipping method
4. Click "Continue to Payment"

**Step 2: Payment Information**
1. Select payment method (Credit Card, PayPal, etc.)
2. Enter payment details securely
3. Review order summary
4. Click "Place Order"

**Step 3: Order Confirmation**
1. Receive order confirmation screen
2. Check your email for order confirmation
3. Order tracking information will be provided

### 2.5 My Account

**Managing Account Information:**
- Click "My Account" or profile icon
- Update personal information
- Change password
- Manage saved addresses
- View order history
- Update notification preferences

**Order Tracking:**
1. Navigate to "My Orders"
2. View order status (Processing, Shipped, Delivered, etc.)
3. Track shipment with provided tracking number
4. Contact support if needed

### 2.6 Wishlist

**Adding to Wishlist:**
- Click the heart icon on any product
- Product will be saved to your wishlist
- Access wishlist from your profile

**Managing Wishlist:**
- View all wishlist items
- Move items to cart
- Remove items from wishlist
- Share wishlist with others

### 2.7 Reviews and Ratings

**Writing a Review:**
1. Navigate to product page
2. Click "Write a Review" button
3. Select star rating (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review title and description
5. Click "Submit Review"

**Managing Reviews:**
- Edit or delete your reviews in "My Reviews"
- Reviews are subject to moderation

## Seller Guide

### 3.1 Seller Registration

**Becoming a Seller:**
1. Log in to your customer account
2. Navigate to "Become a Seller" in your profile
3. Fill out seller application form:
   - Business name
   - Business email
   - Business address
   - Business phone number
   - Tax ID
   - Business description
4. Submit application for review
5. Wait for admin approval (typically 1-3 business days)

### 3.2 Seller Dashboard

**Accessing Seller Dashboard:**
- Log in to your account
- Click "Seller Dashboard" from main menu
- View seller-specific tools and analytics

**Dashboard Overview:**
- Sales performance metrics
- Order notifications
- Inventory status
- Revenue summary
- Account information

### 3.3 Managing Products

**Adding New Products:**
1. In Seller Dashboard, click "Add Product"
2. Fill in product details:
   - Product name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - SKU
   - Inventory quantity
   - Product images
   - Specifications
3. Click "Publish Product"

**Editing Products:**
1. Go to "My Products"
2. Find the product you want to edit
3. Click "Edit" button
4. Make desired changes
5. Click "Update Product"

**Managing Inventory:**
- Update stock quantities regularly
- Set low stock alerts
- Manage product variations (size, color, etc.)
- Monitor product performance

### 3.4 Order Management

**Viewing Orders:**
- Access "Orders" section in Seller Dashboard
- Filter orders by status, date, or customer
- View order details including customer information

**Processing Orders:**
1. Click on an order to view details
2. Verify order information
3. Package and ship the product
4. Enter tracking information
5. Update order status to "Shipped"

**Handling Returns/Refunds:**
- Process return requests through the system
- Follow return policy guidelines
- Issue refunds as appropriate
- Update order status accordingly

### 3.5 Seller Analytics

**Sales Reports:**
- Daily, weekly, monthly sales
- Top selling products
- Customer insights
- Revenue reports

**Performance Metrics:**
- Conversion rates

- Product visibility
- Inventory turnover

## Troubleshooting

### 6.1 Common Issues

**Login Problems:**
- Check email and password for typos
- Verify your email is confirmed
- Reset password if needed using "Forgot Password"
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Payment Issues:**
- Verify payment information is correct
- Check with your bank about transaction blocks
- Ensure sufficient funds in account
- Try different payment method
- Contact support if issues persist

**Shopping Cart Problems:**
- Cart items disappearing: Check if items are in stock
- Incorrect pricing: Refresh the page
- Add to cart not working: Clear browser cache
- Cannot remove items: Try different browser

**Product Search Issues:**
- No results found: Try different keywords
- Incorrect products: Use filters to refine search
- Search not working: Check internet connection
- Results not sorted correctly: Refresh page

### 6.2 Browser Compatibility

**Recommended Browsers:**
- Chrome: Latest 2 versions
- Firefox: Latest 2 versions
- Safari: Latest 2 versions
- Edge: Latest 2 versions

**Browser Settings Required:**
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled
- Pop-ups allowed from our domain
- Updated browser for best experience

### 6.3 Mobile App Troubleshooting

**App Won't Install:**
- Check device compatibility
- Ensure sufficient storage space
- Try different internet connection
- Contact support for assistance

**App Crashes:**
- Update to latest app version
- Clear app cache
- Restart your device
- Reinstall the app if needed

## FAQ

### 7.1 Account Management

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to "My Account" > "Settings" and update your email. Verification will be required.

**Q: How do I close my account?**
A: Contact support to request account closure. Note that this action is permanent.

### 7.2 Orders and Payments

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping takes 1-3 business days.

**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: Contact support within 30 days of delivery to initiate a return request. Items must be in original condition.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, contact support immediately if your order hasn't shipped yet.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other regional payment methods.

### 7.3 Shopping Experience

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Go to "My Orders" in your account or use the tracking link in your order confirmation email.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No, all fees including shipping and taxes are displayed before checkout.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, we ship to select international locations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

### 7.4 Sellers

**Q: How do I become a seller?**
A: Go to "Become a Seller" in your account menu and complete the application.

**Q: What are the seller fees?**
A: The platform takes a commission of X% on each sale. Detailed fee structure is available in seller terms.

**Q: How do I get paid as a seller?**
A: Payments are processed weekly to your connected bank account.
