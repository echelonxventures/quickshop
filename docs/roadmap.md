# QuickShop - Product Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the strategic direction and planned features for QuickShop, a futuristic, scalable, AI-powered e-commerce platform. The roadmap is organized into short-term (0-6 months), medium-term (6-12 months), and long-term (12+ months) objectives aligned with business goals and market opportunities.

## Vision

To create the most intelligent, personalized, and seamless e-commerce experience powered by AI and modern technologies.
